About IRS

IRS (Issue Reporting System) is a crowdsourced platform that bridges the gap between everyday citizens and government agencies. We believe that every citizen has the power to make their city better by being the eyes and ears of their community.

Our platform enables quick reporting, community validation, and transparent tracking of civic issues, making cities safer, cleaner, and more accountable.
